Adapter Plate – BOLT

Compatible with our KeyCom® BOLT intercoms, this 14″ square adapter plate is designed for surface mounting the touch panel unit on generic pedestal faceplates or other surfaces. #304 Stainless steel polished with a brushed #4 finish.
